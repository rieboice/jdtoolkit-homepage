This assignment is meant to familiarize us with manipulating photographs so we can make them work for us in whatever context we need them to - including for this website!

This is a photo from my neighborhood where I selected a shadow on my phone when taking the photo to make the exposure adjust too low. I brightened it in Photoshop to make it more "readable."
